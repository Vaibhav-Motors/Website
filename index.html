About Us

A journey from a roadside garage to a trusted premium car service station.

VAIBHAV MOTOR'S

Started in March 2021 as a humble roadside garage, we have grown into a fully equipped car service station. This journey was only possible with the blessings of God and the unwavering trust and support of our dear customers.

In our constant effort to serve you better, we have brought comprehensive auto solutions under one roof. From General Servicing to complex Transmission, Steering, Engine, and Electrical work — we handle it all.

We also specialize in Vehicle Diagnostics, Brake & Clutch Systems, Body Shop repairs, Accidental Vehicle restoration, Denting, Painting, and Polishing. Earning customer confidence and satisfaction is our ultimate goal.
